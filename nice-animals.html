The Wombat

★ ★ ★ ★

I'm surprised that this is a real animal. Nonetheless, it is at the pinnacle of evolution. The only reason it is not 5 stars is because I have yet to see one in real life to confirm its existence. This looks like the perfect animal if domesticated. It is one of the rare, non-demonic animals to come out of Australia.

The Komodo Dragon

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

This is the true king of beasts. I've seen a video of one devouring a full-on gazelle, and it was awesome. This thing could breathe fire, and I would not be surprised. The tongue is menacing as well. They can also run very fast.

The Dog

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Obviously, the dog comes in at 5 stars. It is the pinnacle of domesticated animals. It has no flaws. This is a photo of Barack Obama's dog.

The Cat

★ ★ ★ ★

The cat can either be a great or terrible pet. Some are very sociable while others are lame. They also defy physics at times which is pretty neat. I have heard that outdoor cats form gangs that spread across different cities. They also have a surprisingly long life-span.

The Rat

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Expert survivalist, the rat has served as an example for what all species should strive to achieve: survival by any means necessary. I've never met anyone that grinds harder than the rat. The rat is persistent does what it needs to in order to accomplish its goals. It is a good metaphor for life.

Stegosaurus

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

I have to give credit where credit is due. The stegosaurus remains as part of the starting lineup in the animal kingdom. It has literal armor, and it could probably use its scales to absorb sunlight and charge its phone and stuff. There is a reason why the stegosaurus has remained alive after all these centuries, and it is because it is one of the greats.

The Snake

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

The snake is the original villain of the animal kingdom and The Bible I think. However, it is definitely one of the best written villains in literature. If I died by getting eaten by a snake, it would have been a good death.

Centaur

★ ★ ★ ★

This is technically only half animal, but it definitely deserves to be on the list. They guard the forests at night, and they protect the United States from international terrorism threats. I don't like most horses though.

Anteater

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Genuinely, one of the most innovative creations of all time. They are one of the most sophisticated beasts of the land, and they deserve our respect for the work that they do. They also have a fascinating fur pattern, and are much larger than most people think. They look wise. They remind me of Oogway for some reason.

Mamoswine
